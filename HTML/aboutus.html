"Our Story: Born from Innovation"

Red Bull was created in 1987 by Dietrich Mateschitz, an Austrian entrepreneur, and Chaleo Yoovidhya, a Thai businessman. The duo saw the potential to bring an energy drink to the global market that would appeal to active, ambitious individuals. Mateschitz adapted Chaleo's formula for the Western market, modifying its taste and branding to suit consumer preferences. The result was Red Bull Energy Drink, a revolutionary product that provided an immediate energy boost through a unique combination of caffeine, taurine, and B-vitamins
